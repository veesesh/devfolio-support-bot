## **Step 1**

* Navigate to our organizer Dashboard at [https://org.devfolio.co](https://org.devfolio.co/)
* Look for the "Organize New" button and give it a click. This is where your journey begins!
* Setting up a hackathon tailored for your audience is crucial. Click on "Start Hackathon Setup for Students" to begin customizing the experience.
* Choose the desired 'Hackathon Mode'. Not sure which mode suits you best? No worries! Click [here](/docs/guide/modes) for a comprehensive guide.

<img src="/assets/Step 1.gif" alt="Start hackathon for students" />

## **Step 2**

* Begin by filling out all necessary details. Once you've covered all the bases and the indicator gleams at 100%, click "Finish Setup".

<Callout type="info" title="Need help with setup?">
  For detailed steps on filling in the hackathon organizer application, refer to [Setting up your Hackathon](setting-up-your-hackathon/).
</Callout>

* Just a few final touches:
  * **Discord Setup:** Create a dedicated channel on the Devfolio server to ensure prompt support.
  * **MoU Procedure:** An MoU will be signed between Devfolio and the organizing party.
  * **Brand Integration:** Add the "Apply with Devfolio" button to your hackathon website.

## **Step 3**

Click on "Submit for Verification" once you're ready.

## **Step 4**

* Once we've validated the details and confirmed legitimacy, your hackathon will be verified.
* After verification, it will go live on [https://devfolio.co/hackathons](https://devfolio.co/hackathons).
