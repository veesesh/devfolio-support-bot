<Callout title="Permission Required" type="warning">
  The winners can be added only by the <strong>creator</strong> of the hackathon.
</Callout>

## 🪄 Steps to Announce Winners on Devfolio

### Step 1: Login

Login as an Organizer on Devfolio  
[https://org.devfolio.co](https://org.devfolio.co)

### Step 2: Click on the Edit Button

Navigate to your hackathon card and click the <code>Edit</code> button.

<img src="/assets/image (1) (1) (1) (1) (1) (1) (1) (1) (1).webp" alt="Edit Hackathon" />
<p align="center"><i>Edit hackathon to update winners</i></p>

### Step 3: Update Winners from the Prizes Tab

<img src="/assets/pika-1693121665130-1x.webp" alt="Add winners to prize" />
<p align="center"><i>Select prize and add winning teams or projects</i></p>

You can:

- Click on <code>+ Add winners</code> to an existing prize category  
- Type the <strong>project name</strong> or <strong>team name</strong> to assign the winner


## 🌐 Microsite View

<Callout title="Info" type="info">
  Participants can view the winning projects from both the <code>Prizes</code> and <code>Projects</code> tabs.
</Callout>
