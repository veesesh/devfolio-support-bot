---
title: Feedback Tab
description: The hackers will be able to submit feedback to your hackathon, and the Feedback Tab helps you keep track of that.
---

<Callout title="Note" type="info">
  The Feedback Tab is visible <strong>after the end of your hackathon's submission deadline</strong>
</Callout>

<img src="/assets/image (168).webp" alt="Feedback Tab Screenshot" />
<p align="center"><i>Feedback responses panel</i></p>

- You can send reminder emails by clicking on the `Send Reminder Emails` button
- You can find two separate tabs:
  - **Response:** Participants respond to standard questions post the hackathon.
  - **Review:** Overall reviews from the participant.
