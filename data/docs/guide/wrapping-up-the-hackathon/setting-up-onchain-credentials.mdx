## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Go to Organizer Dashboard

Click on your profile in the top-right corner and select <code>Organizer Dashboard</code>.

<img src="/assets/image (183).webp" alt="Access Organizer Dashboard" />
<p align="center"><i>Access your Organizer Dashboard</i></p>



### 2. Enable Onchain Credentials in Brand Tab

Navigate to the <code>Brand</code> tab of your hackathon and enable Onchain Credentials.

<img src="/assets/image (184).webp" alt="Enable Onchain Credentials" />
<p align="center"><i>Enable Onchain Credentials toggle</i></p>



### 3. Fill in Collection Details

Complete the collection information and select the intended receiver.

<img src="/assets/image (185).webp" alt="Enter credential details" />
<p align="center"><i>Set collection name and recipient</i></p>



### 4. Customize and Upload

Preview the credential template or adjust its colors, then click <code>Upload</code>.

<img src="/assets/image (186).webp" alt="Upload Onchain Credential design" />
<p align="center"><i>Customize and upload your credential</i></p>



### 5. Add Custom Onchain Credentials

For volunteers or guests not listed in the dashboard, you can issue custom credentials.

<img src="/assets/image (187).webp" alt="Add custom Onchain credentials" />
<p align="center"><i>Manually add credentials for guests or volunteers</i></p>
