---
title: Claiming your Schwag
description: Step-by-step walkthrough to claim your Devfolio schwag after participating in a hackathon.
---



### 1. Go to **My Claims**

Click on your profile in the top-right corner and choose **"My Claims"**  
or directly visit [devfolio.co/claims](https://devfolio.co/claims)

<p><img src="/assets/image 135 (1) (1).webp" alt="My Claims" /></p>


### 2. Select the **Schwag** Card

<p><img src="/assets/image 133 (1).webp" alt="Schwag category card" /></p>


### 3. Click **Claim Schwag**

Find the eligible schwag and click **Claim schwag**.

<p><img src="/assets/pika-1682066192886-1x 1.webp" alt="Claim schwag button" /></p>


### 4. Fill Out the Shipping Form

Enter the required details and submit the form.

<p><img src="/assets/pika-1682066306949-1x 1.webp" alt="Shipping form" /></p>
