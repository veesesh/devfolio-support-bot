title: Claiming your Onchain Credentials
description: Step-by-step guide to claim your Soulbound Token (SBT) on Devfolio.
---

<Callout type="info" title="Know more about Onchain Credentials">
Know more about onchain credentials by <a href="/docs/guide/onchain-credentials">clicking here</a>.
</Callout>

1. Click on your profile in the top-right corner and choose 'My Claims' or click <a href="https://devfolio.co/claims">here</a>.

<img src="/assets/image 135 (1) (2).webp" alt="Navigate to My Claims" />
<p align="center"><i>Navigate to My Claims</i></p>

2. Select the **Onchain Credentials** card.

<img src="/assets/image 133.webp" alt="Onchain credentials card" />
<p align="center"><i>Onchain credentials card</i></p>

3. Choose the credential that you want to claim.

<img src="/assets/image 137 (3) (1) (1).webp" alt="Select credential" />
<p align="center"><i>Select the credential to claim</i></p>

4. Link your Ethereum wallet to Devfolio.

<img src="/assets/image 139.webp" alt="Link Ethereum wallet" />
<p align="center"><i>Connect your wallet</i></p>

5. Click on **Continue with Ethereum** and finish the transaction.

<img src="/assets/image 146 (1).webp" alt="Confirm transaction" />
<p align="center"><i>Continue with Ethereum</i></p>

6. Double-check the wallet details and click on **Confirm Wallet**.  
   For `gasless` Onchain Credentials, the transaction is executed by Devfolio.

<img src="/assets/image (40).webp" alt="Confirm wallet" />
<p align="center"><i>Confirm wallet and complete transaction</i></p>

   You can view it on OpenSea & EAS Scan and tweet about it!

<img src="/assets/image (41).webp" alt="SBT claimed success screen" />
<p align="center"><i>Your credential is now live onchain</i></p>
