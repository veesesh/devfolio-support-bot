## Steps to set up a new hackathon

1. Go to [https://org.devfolio.co/](https://org.devfolio.co/).  
2. Click on **Organize New**.  
3. Choose the hackathon type:  
   - **Students** → Schools or Universities  
   - **Communities/Companies**  
4. Click on the respective **Start Hackathon Setup**.  
5. Select the hackathon **mode** (online, offline, or hybrid).  
6. Fill in the basic details:  
   - Hackathon name  
   - Organization/University you are representing  
   - Other introductory information  
7. Click **Begin** to start the setup.  

---

## Tabs to configure your hackathon

Once inside the setup, configure each tab to define your hackathon experience:

- **Basics** – General details of your hackathon. These details will be visible on the hosted subdomain once the hackathon is live.  
- **Applications** – Select the profile fields you wish to make mandatory in your hackathon application. The shorter your form, the faster you’ll stack up applications.  
- **Links** – Add relevant links such as your website, socials, or community pages.  
- **Brand** – Customize your hackathon microsite to match your branding.  
- **Dates** – Define important timelines and schedules for your hackathon.  
- **Prizes** – Use this tab to add prizes. This is also the best way to introduce **track prizes**, so that if a hacker builds a project for a specific track, they can submit it to that track.  
- **Judges** – Add judges and define evaluation criteria.  
- **Partners** – Showcase your sponsors and partners.  
- **FAQs** – Address common participant queries to reduce repetitive questions.
