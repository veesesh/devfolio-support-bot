---
title: Brand Tab
description: Make your hackathon microsite to match your brand.
---

You can add your Hackathon Logo, as well as the Cover Image in this tab. In order to make it more customisable, you can enter the hex code for your brand colour, which will be added as a background colour on your hackathon's dashboard.

<img src="/assets/image (56).webp" alt="Organizer view of brand tab" />

## Dimensions Required (for Optimum Results)

- **Cover Image**: 776 × 437  
- **Hackathon Logo**: 300 × 124  
- **Hackathon Favicon**: 192 × 192
